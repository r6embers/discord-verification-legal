Privacy Policy – Discord Verification Bot

Effective Date: [Enter Date]

This Privacy Policy describes how the Discord Verification Bot handles your data.

  1. Information Collected
    We collect:
    • Your Discord user ID
    • Basic public profile info from the platform you choose to verify with (TikTok, Twitch, YouTube)
  2. How We Use It
    This information is only used to:
    • Match social media accounts to your Discord ID
    • Assign roles in participating Discord servers
  3. Data Sharing
    We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. Data is only visible to the server administrators of the server you verified in.
  4. Storage & Security
    Data is stored securely and used only in real time to validate the verification. No long-term storage is performed unless explicitly configured by the server owner.
  5. Your Rights
    You may request deletion of your data by contacting the bot administrator.

If you have questions or concerns, please reach out to the server moderators where the bot is active.
